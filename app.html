When2Play App

These are the various screens from our mobile app

The home screen allows the user to:

1) first pick the recreational activity they are interested in.

2) The next screen lists parks near your location with that recreational activity.

3) After selecting a park, the third screen provides specific real-time information as to the condition of the park facilities using our sensors.

4) The final screen shows a possible customizable notification feature that will alert the user based on his or her preferences.
