Our Solution involves a network of very cheap, easy-to-install, and easily replaceable arduino computers that will be able to detect the wetness, temperature and humidity of courts, playgrounds and any other parks facilities.

How does it work?

One of our sensors will be placed at various locations around the park. Each sensor costs only 5 Dollars. The sensors will relay real-time information to a central server located in the park office, or enclosed station. This will then be easily accessible using our mobile application
