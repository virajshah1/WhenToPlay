What was the Inspiration?

Our political science Professor, Dr. Jennifer Connolly, introduced a new class known as MuniMod. MuniMod is a state-wide hack-a-thon type of competition hosted by the Florida League of Cities for college students to develop an innovative solution that could be used by local municipalities throughout the state of Florida.
